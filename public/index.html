Water Quality Guidelines

Temperature

Safe Range: 24-30°C
Recommended: 26-28°C

pH Level

Safe Range: 6.8-7.8
Recommended: 7.2-7.6

Chlorine

Safe Range: 1.0-2.0 ppm
Recommended: 1.2-1.8 ppm

Why These Matter?

• Temperature affects comfort and safety
• pH balance prevents skin irritation
• Chlorine keeps water clean and safe
